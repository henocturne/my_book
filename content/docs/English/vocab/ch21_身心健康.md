### 二、身心健康 (Chapter 21)
该章节涵盖了人体结构、生理功能、疾病症状、医疗手段以及心理情感等词汇。

*   **人体构造（外部与骨骼）：** human (人类), bone (骨), backbone (脊柱), skeleton (骨骼), skull (头骨), brain (脑), cheek (面颊), forehead (额), brow (眉), eyelash (眼睫毛), lip (嘴唇), mouth (口), tongue (舌), throat (喉咙), chin (下巴), jaw (颌), neck (脖子), shoulder (肩), limb (四肢), elbow (肘), wrist (腕), thumb (拇指), palm (手掌), finger (手指), fist (拳头), chest (胸), abdomen (腹), rib (肋骨), waist (腰), thigh (大腿), joint (关节), knee (膝盖), ankle (踝), heel (足跟), toe (脚趾)。
*   **人体器官与生理：** organ (器官), liver (肝脏), stomach (胃), womb (子宫), kidney (肾脏), lung (肺), gland (腺), intestine (肠), muscle (肌肉), pore (毛孔), pulse (脉搏), artery (动脉), nerve (神经), hormone (荷尔蒙), immune (免疫的), sense (感官), sight/eyesight (视力)。
*   **疾病、症状与状态：** symptom (症状), disease/illness (疾病), infection (传染), dysfunction (功能障碍), diabetes (糖尿病), obesity (肥胖症), overweight (超重的), hypertension (高血压), paralyse (瘫痪), dizzy (眩晕), insane (精神错乱的), insomnia (失眠), allergy (过敏), heart attack (心脏病发作), cancer (癌), arthritis (关节炎), stroke (中风), plague (瘟疫), flu (流感), fever (发烧), headache (头痛), swell (肿胀), choke (窒息), injure/hurt (伤害), bleed (流血), bruise (淤伤), scar (疤痕), wound (创伤), trauma (外伤/精神创伤), pain (疼痛), death (死亡), fatal/lethal (致命的), exhaust/fatigue (疲劳)。
*   **医疗与护理：** hospital (医院), therapy (治疗), health care (医疗保健), sanitary (卫生的), medical (医学的), hygiene (卫生), clinic (诊所), physician (内科医生), surgeon (外科医生), operation (手术), cure/heal (治愈), treatment (治疗), prescription (处方), transplant (移植), X-ray (X光), injection (注射), vaccinate (接种疫苗), pharmacy/medicine (药物)。
*   **心理与情感：** mood (心情), emotion (情绪), attitude (态度), character/personality (性格/个性), happiness (幸福), delight/joy (快乐), thrill (激动), cheer (振奋), depression (沮丧), anxiety (焦虑), fear (害怕), panic (惊恐), stress/tension (压力/紧张), confidence (自信), sympathy (同情), gratitude (感激), pride (骄傲), anger (愤怒), hate/hatred (仇恨), jealousy (嫉妒), guilt (内疚), regret/repent (后悔/忏悔), shame (羞愧)。
*   **性格特征描述：** ambitious (有雄心的), optimistic (乐观的), pessimistic (悲观的), enthusiastic (热情的), indifferent (漠不关心的), honest (诚实的), polite (有礼貌的), stubborn (固执的), humble (谦虚的), generous (慷慨的), sensitive (敏感的), naive (天真的)。
